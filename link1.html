Carrot Cake

Ingredients Amount
Flour 2 1/2 cup
Baking powder 1 1/4 tsp
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Cinnamon 1 1/4 tsp
Nutmeg 1/2 tsp
Cloves 1/8 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Eggs 4 (large)
Sugar 1 1/2 cup
Brown Sugar 1/2 cup (packed)
Vegetable Oil 1 1/2 cup
Carrots 1 pound (peeled and grated)

Instructions

  • Heat oven to 350 degrees
  • Grease a 9 * 13 inch cake pan and line the bottom with parchment paper
  • Whisk together the flour, baking poweder, baking soda, spices, and salt in a large bowl and set aside
  • Whisk together the eggs and sugar for about one minute
  • Slowly add in the oil
  • Whisk in the floir mixture until combined
  • Stir in the Carrots
  • Pour batter into the pan and smooth the top
  • Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, turning halfway through
  • Let the cake cool in the pan for 2 hours
